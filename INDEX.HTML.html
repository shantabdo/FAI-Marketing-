1. Multi-Channel Campaign Strategy

Target Audience Analysis

Primary Demographics

  • Household income: $100,000+
  • Children aged 18 months - 25 years
  • Multiple or severe food allergies
  • Previous treatment failures (OIT)
  • National/international geographic spread
  • Willing to travel to California for treatment

Emotional & Behavioral Profile

  • Constant anxiety about child's safety
  • Social isolation due to food restrictions
  • Hypervigilant about food exposure
  • Desperate for permanent solution
  • High engagement with support communities
  • Read every food label multiple times

Channel Budget Allocation

Paid Search: 35%
Social Media: 30%
Programmatic: 20%
Retargeting: 15%

Channel Strategy Rationale

Paid Search (35%): High-intent keywords when parents actively seek solutions for worsening allergies. Focus on "food allergy cure" and "eliminate food allergies".

Social Media (30%): Strong parent support communities where word-of-mouth is crucial. Target food allergy support groups vs general parenting.

Programmatic (20%): Reach parents on medical websites and allergy-related content consumption.

Retargeting (15%): Multiple touchpoints needed for trust-building in this high-stakes audience.

2. Lead Qualification Framework

MQL Criteria (75+ Points)

Behavioral Scoring:

  • Consultation form submission: +50 points
  • Patient guide download: +25 points
  • Success story video completion: +20 points
  • Pricing page visit: +30 points
  • Multiple page visits: +5 points each

Demographic Scoring:

  • Multiple allergies indicated: +35 points
  • Previous OIT failure: +40 points
  • High-income zip code: +20 points
  • Target age range (18mo-25yr): +30 points

SQL Criteria (125+ Points + Consultation)

BANT Qualification:

  • Budget: $1,100+ monthly capacity ($30k total)
  • Authority: Both parents as decision-makers
  • Need: Severe allergies impacting quality of life
  • Timeline: Ready to start within 6 months

Additional Qualification:

  • Confirmed severe food allergies (anaphylactic reactions)
  • Willing to travel to California 4-6x annually
  • Consultation call completed (80%+ attendance rate)
  • Expressed interest in clinic visit scheduling
